    Project Idea

    ParKd

    Overview

    ParKd, A motorcycle app for Sydney.

    A free, community-based user rated two wheeled parking location app. Providing a platform for locating the closest community rated parking near your chosen location.

    Motivation

    Personally, as a two wheeled commuter living in Sydney this app is needed.

    Parking is in high demand in Sydney between residents, workers and soon to be visitors who ride. Finding a safe place to park is not always possible and it is a timely expense that could and should be mitigated.

    Description

    ParKd is an application for people who ride everyday within the city as well as those who are visiting for different purposes or just moving into the city.

    Personally, I have been struggling to find a park place ever since I have moved to Sydney. Looking for a place where I can park securely on two wheels might not also be safe for the others in traffic as well. Another issue is that my bike has been hit and broken in one of those areas. Hence why I would like to know how safe and secure these places are as an outsider. Having this app would make life much easier for me and other two-wheel commuters.

    Building a mobile application with use of geoprocessing, routing, and mapping.

    It is essential that ParKd has a user-friendly interface, in a little three clicks you have your space.

  • When the user opens the app, they are presented with approximate location and a search box to enter their desired parking location.
  • The user is then presented with the alternative parking locations with user safety ratings.
  • The user will be asked to pick out of five stars how happy they were to leave their vehicle to provide insight for the other users.

    • Tools and Technologies

    the following technologies will be needed

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Java

    • Skills Required

    Strong understanding of GIS technologies specifically ArcGIS Runtime API for Java.

    Outcome

    ParKd, will provide timely motorcycle parking solutions to address the issue of reliable parking in Sydney.
